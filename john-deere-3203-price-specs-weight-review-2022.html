John Deere 3203 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 3TNV84 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 91.5 ci [1.5 L] engine, which delivers 32 hp [23.9 kW] power at rated rpm 2800.

John Deere 3203 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Yanmar 1.5L 3-cyl diesel engine,  4×4 MFWD 4WD, infinite (2-range) forward and reverse gears, 4.5 qts [4.3 L] oil capacity, power steering, wet disc brakes and 2149 lbs [974 kg] Rear lift (at ends).

John Deere 3203 Compact Utility tractor attachments include John Deere 300 front-end loader.
