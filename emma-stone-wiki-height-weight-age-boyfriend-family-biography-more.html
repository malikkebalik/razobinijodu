In 2004, Emma made her TV debut through an American TV series “The Partridge Family”. Late she did minor roles for TV shows like “Malcolm in the Middle” and “Drive”. In 2008, she sang her first song “I know what boys like” in the movie “The House Bunny”. She earned more popularity and appreciation for her role in “The Amazing Spider-man”. She was featured as Parker’s girlfriend “Gwen Stacy”.

She also appeared in the sequel of the movie. In 2016, she was starred in the musical comedy “La La Land”. The movie was a huge success and received many Oscar awards in 2017. Emma was honored with Oscar for “Best Actress” in La La Land.
