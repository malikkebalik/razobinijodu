Tuesday, October 8th, My Big Fat Revenge will be returning for its 6th episode in its first season. If there’s one thing we’ve come to understand over the course of this first season it’s that revenge comes in all types, in all forms, and in all varying degrees of craziness. On tonight’s episode, Niasia wants to confront her father about the things he used to say about her weight and Paula — who also struggles with her weight — wants to get some healthy revenge on her business partner, Anthony.

We’ve been getting our fair share of revenge on this first season of My Big Fat Revenge, and if you think the sassy drama is going to let up anytime soon, well, then you’re sorely mistaken.
