Idara Victor is an American actress. She had her career beginnings in the mid-2000s. During this period, she made one-time roles on such shows as Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and Law & Order. She continued making guest appearances in the 2010s on The Young and the Restless, How to Make It in America, Prime Suspect, and Private Practice. She next appeared in the movie Watching TV with the Red Chinese. In 2013, she starred as Miriam on the web series The Choir. She next starred as Nina Holiday on the TNT series Rizzoli & Isles, which gained her recognition. At the same time, she played Abigail on the show Turn: Washington’s Spies. She will appear in the upcoming movie Alita: Battle Angel.
