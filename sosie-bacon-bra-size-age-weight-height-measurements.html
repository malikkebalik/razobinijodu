Biography:

Sosie Bacon was born on March 15, 1992 in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of actors Kevin Bacon and Kyra Sedgwick. In 2005, she made her acting debut in the film Loverboy. Four years later, she played Charlie Johnson in 4 episodes of The Closer, in which her mother starred in. She became more active in the 2010s, first playing the recurring role of Rachel Murray in Scream.

She next appeared in the film Ana Maria in Novela Land. From 2017 to 2018, she played Skye Miller in Netflix’s 13 Reasons Why. Following this, she portrayed Patricia Krenwinkel in the biographical film Charlie Says. This year, she played Audrey in the Netflix film The Last Summer. She also starred as Kristen in the short-lived HBO series Here and Now.
