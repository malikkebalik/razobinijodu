Who is the Captain of the USA Ryder Cup?

Zach Johnson is the captain of the USA Ryder Cup. Team USA captain Zach Johnson was free to continue strategizing how his team would win the Ryder Cup on European territory for the first time in 30 years while the European camp coped with the upheaval of losing the leader.

Johnson has previously worked as a vice-captain twice, once for Steve Stricker in Whistling Straits and once for Jim Furyk in Paris in 2018. Additionally, he participated in five Ryder Cups losing the first four before winning at Hazeltine in 2016.
