John Deere 4755 Row-Crop Tractor is equipped with a John Deere turbocharged diesel 6-cylinder liquid-cooled inline 466 ci [7.6 L] engine, which delivers 151 hp [112.6 kW] power at rated rpm 2200.

John Deere 4755 Row-Crop Tractor featured with a John Deere 7.6L 6-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, hydraulic wet disc brakes, 16200 to 18165 pounds weight, 5,821 lbs [2640 kg] rear lift, 16 forward and 6 reverse gears and 22 Qts [20.8 L] engine oil capacity.

John Deere 4755 Row-Crop tractor attachments include John Deere 280, and John Deere 840 front-end loader.
