Starting with this, Who is Jelena Djokovic?

Serb businesswoman and humanitarian Jelena Djokovic, born in Belgrade in 1986, currently serves as the NDF’s global CEO. She co-founded the organization with her husband to promote equal access to early childhood education.

In addition, Jelena oversees Original Magazine, a website, and blog that creates community-focused material as part of the NDF. Additionally, she has a Master’s in Luxury Goods and Services from the International University of Monaco and a Bachelor’s in Business Administration and Management from Milan’s famed Bocconi University.

Aside from maintaining a blog, she hasn’t held since 2020, Jelena is also highly active on social media, frequently posting images of Novak and their family to her 616,000 Instagram followers.

Why Tom Brady Sits Next to Jelena Djokovic?

Tom Brady, a seven-time Super Bowl champion, was photographed on Sunday watching the men’s 2023 French Open final from Novak Djokovic’s box with his wife, Jelena. At Roland Garros in Paris, France’s Philippe-Chatrier court was the site of the match.

Djokovic aims to surpass Rafael Nadal’s existing record of holding the most men’s singles majors by winning his 23rd Grand Slam title. The former quarterback and the Serbian tennis player have a long-standing bond, and Djokovic has said that he routinely consults Brady for tips on training and rehabilitation.

After defeating Casper Ruud in the 34th Grand Slam final of his career, Djokovic captured his third French Open crown. Djokovic was the first to win at least three titles at each of the four major tournaments. Soccer players Kylian Mbappe and Zlatan Ibrahimovic were among the other stars in attendance to witness Djokovic’s victory.
