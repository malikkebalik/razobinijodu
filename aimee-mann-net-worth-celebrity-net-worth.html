What is Aimee Mann's Net Worth?

Aimee Mann is an American singer and musician who has a net worth of $10 million. Aimee Mann was born in Richmond, Virginia in September 1960. She plays bass guitar, guitar, percussion, and keyboards and her styles include rock, folk, and alternative. Mann served as the vocalist and bassist for the band 'Til Tuesday. The band formed in 1982 and broke up in 1989. Their debut studio album Voices Carry reached #19 on the Billboard 200 chart in 1985.

The band also released the albums Welcome Home in 1986 and Everything's Different Now in 1988. 'Til Tuesday's single "Voices Carry" reached #8 on the US Hot 100 chart and #14 on the US Mainstream Rock chart. Mann released her solo debut studio album Whatever in 1993. She released the albums I'm with Stupid in 1995, Bachelor No. 2 or, the Last Remains of the Dodo in 2000, Lost in Space in 2002, The Forgotten Arm in 2005, One More Drifter in the Snow in 2006, @#%&*! Smilers in 2008, Charmer in 2012, and Mental Illness in 2017. Her song "Save Me" was nominated for two Grammy Awards, a Golden Globe Award, and an Academy Award.
