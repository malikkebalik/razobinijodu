Gaetz can always count on his communications director Joel Valdez, who started working for him in 2021, despite his many detractors. This is all the information we have gathered thus far about him.

Who is Joel Valdez Gaetz?

Since April 2021, Valdez has been working as Gaetz’s director of communications. He was born on February 11 making him about 23 years old when he finished high school in 2017. He attended and graduated from UIC College Prep.
