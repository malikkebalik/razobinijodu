Biography:

Lauren Collins was born on August 29, 1986 in Thornhill, Ontario, Canada. Her acting career began in 1998 as a child actress, debuting in the film Valentine’s Day. That same year, she also made brief appearances in some TV shows, such as Real Kids, Real Adventures. After this, she starred as Lindy May in the television series I Was a Sixth Grade Alien.

During her teens, she starred as Brooke Lanier in the show In a Heartbeat. Collins then rose to fame for starring as Paige Michalchuk in the teen drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation. Around this period, she also had performances in the movies Take the Lead, Charlie Bartlett, and Picture This. The actress also played Alicia O’Sullivan in the TV drama The Best Years. In 2018, she appeared as Meghan Lingerman, a recurring character in the sci-fi drama Impulse.
